You are more than a number!

Providing the tailored support you need to get healthy and keep the weight off

Nightingale Aesthetics recognizes that weight loss involves much more than just shedding extra pounds. We understand that achieving and maintaining a healthy weight goes beyond appearance, as important as it is for your self-image. When we collaborate with our friends and neighbors in Staunton, Virginia, to help them reach and sustain a healthy weight, our team plays a vital role in promoting their overall wellness, well-being, health, and quality of life.

You are reading this for a reason, and we want to hear from you so that we can offer our support. This begins with getting to know you as a person beyond just a number on a scale. That number doesn't define you. You are a collection of life experiences, a unique individual with distinct hopes and aspirations. Our personalized approach takes all of this into account.

How do I get started?

Look, we know your struggles did not occur overnight. You are already taking that big first step by exploring your options to get healthy responsibly and sustainably. We congratulate you on doing this. The next step would be to reach out to our team. We'll schedule an initial visit for you with one of our nurse practitioners, Beth Davis or Janna Shields. During your visit, we will review many different things that reflect your daily life – your habits, behaviors, choices, overall health status (including any medications you may be taking), and more. From there, we can create a customized plan for you and you only. Yes, no two treatment plans are the same because we recognize and factor in your one-of-a-kind schedule, food preferences, health history, sleep patterns, and overall lifestyle, among other aspects of your life that can influence weight loss/management.

I know every plan is different, but what might mine include?

You're right – what we recommend for you can differ considerably from what may be recommended for a family member, a member of your congregation, or a friend or colleague. However, we can confidently say there are some general things that many clients should expect. For example, you should expect accountability. We will check in with you regularly. We want to see how things are going, along with recommendations like physical activity and nutrition. Staying on track is important; having a true partner who can hold you accountable helps with that. It's like having a fun, friendly, and inspirational coach on your side. You should also expect regular check-ins virtually or at our office. Your time is important to you and to us. There is a very valid and important place for certain weight loss medications that are appropriate for you and tailored to your needs.

Why can't I just take the injections (Ozempic®)?

Those injections are not right for everyone. Plus, even if we do find that you are a good candidate for injectable medications that help with hunger, it's not a permanent answer. You need to incorporate lifestyle changes along with those injections. Experienced NPs, Janna and Beth, and our team emphasize that there is more to medical weight loss than injectable medications. As needed, there are other medications, some of which can be taken in pill form and have been proven to help shed the lbs. Vitamins like B12 can be incorporated, too.

We take a multidisciplinary approach as well, working with the likes of primary care physicians to help keep clients' diabetes in check and to reduce the risk of developing these and other serious medical conditions. So many problems affecting your quality of life are rooted in excess weight. So, yes, we know it's important for you to feel good about your appearance and to fit into your favorite clothes again. However, it is crucial to see the bigger picture, which involves achieving and maintaining true health in mind, body, and spirit.

So, I won't just gain back all the weight I lost?

Correct – that's the idea with medically-managed weight loss. Too often, we fall prey to fad diets or yo-yo dieting. When we inevitably start incorporating more foods into our diet again, we regain the lost weight and often more than we had before we started. That's because we did not make those little changes that contribute to sustained health and weight maintenance. With patience, you can fit into jeans again while feeling increasingly energized, better rested, and more confident in your skin.
